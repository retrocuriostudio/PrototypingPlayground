Finland Tmi B2B Invoicing Breakdown Calculator

Prototype for Finland-based Toiminimi (Tmi) invoicing analysis. It supports a quick estimate, a more tax-aware view, and a reverse solver that estimates the required monthly invoicing needed to reach a target monthly net cash result.

Monthly + annual breakdown Quick estimate + advanced inputs Configurable tax bands and assumptions Explanation view with formulas and source URLs
Important prototype note
This tool is an estimation aid, not tax advice. Every percentage, threshold, band, and assumption can be edited. The explanation panel explicitly labels whether a figure is an input, configurable assumption, or estimate-only output.
